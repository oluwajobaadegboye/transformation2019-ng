Pastor Thompson Aderemi   (Regional Overseer)

When the cry of sinful men and the tears of broken women reach the ears of the Almighty God, a stirring occurs, a calling ensues, and a commissioning breaks forth. The Savior’s voice rings clear: “I have seen the affliction of my people and heard their cry. Now therefore arise…and tell Pharaoh to let my people go”.

Such commissioning resonated within Pastor Thompson Aderemi when he met the Savior in 1970 at 17. The joy of salvation, the knowledge of lost souls’ impending fate and his commitment to promptly obey the Holy Spirit led Pastor Thompson to his current place of immense influence. Upon conversion, Pastor Thompson began extensive work with Scripture Union, Evangelical Churches of West Africa (ECWA), Sudan Interior Missions and the then Deeper Life Bible Study located in Jos (the birthing place of DLBC in Northern Nigeria) before relocating to the United States and embarking upon the life-long mission of church planting, soul shepherding and leadership development.

Since 1985, Pastor Thompson, his beautiful wife, Selinah, and their children have continued trailblazing throughout the United States from the very first Deeper Life Bible Church in Montgomery, Alabama, to one of the Holy Spirit’s most recent endeavors in Reno, Nevada.

Currently, Pastor Thompson serves as the Regional Overseer over the Southwest and Great Plains Regions (Texas, Arizona, Nevada, Oklahoma, New Mexico, North Dakota, South Dakota, Nebraska, Montana, and Kansas) ceaselessly pouring his life into the lives of others.

Truly, “The world has yet to see what God will do with a man who is fully and wholly consecrated to the Holy Spirit” – Henry Varley
